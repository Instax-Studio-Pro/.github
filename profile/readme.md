## ✨ About Instax Studio Pro

**Instax Studio Pro** is a free, fully browser-based photo design tool that lets anyone create professional-quality instant-style photos — no software to install, no account required. Designed with the aesthetic of Fuji Instax film at its heart, it delivers a seamless, studio-grade experience on any device.

> 🎞️ *Built for instant photography enthusiasts, professional photographers, and creative hobbyists alike.*

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

Instax Studio Pro was crafted around a single guiding principle:

> **"Studio quality. Browser simplicity."**

Every design decision — from the **charcoal black** (`#111827`) background and **warm cream** (`#FDF6E3`) typography to the **soft gold accents** (`#C6A75E`) — reflects the warmth and timelessness of analogue instant photography. The glassmorphism UI panels, smooth animations, and subtle film grain textures are all deliberate nods to the Fuji Instax aesthetic.

No frameworks. No accounts. No paywalls.
